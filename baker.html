Picture Lake

0.6 miles roundtrip

Elevation gain: 45 feet

Mount Baker National Park

Mount Baker National Park is probably the most underrated national park in Washington State, and the autumn colors are the best here.
The hues of orange, yellows, reds, and greens were therapeutic to explore in, and the water was crystal clear.
Picture Lake was less of a hike and more of a walk, very high reward for low hiking effort!
Watching the sun set and the pink hues reflect on the mountain was just the most beautiful sight!
