Lodge Lake

3.75 miles roundtrip

Elevation gain: 950 feet

Snoqualmie Pass

This hike is along the Pacific Crest Trail, and it's cool to see the ski lifts in the summer with the flowers blooming beneath.
The actual lake wasn't too much of a speciality, this hike is much more of a journey hike.
There was hella bugs by the lake - highlight was definitely walking along the meadow!
