Lake 22

5.4 miles roundtrip

Elevation gain: 1,350 feet

Mount Baker Snoqualmie National Forest

Lake 22 is one of my favorite hikes in the area - a moderate hike for high reward! Additionally, it is hikeable mostly year round, and the views are so fun and different depending on what season you go. When I went and it was very cloudy in the winter I thought we would not have a good time at the alpine lake, but we ended up having an amazing time walking around on the frozen lake within the clouds. In the summer, we glacial plunged into the lake - Lake 22 is always one of my favorite hikes in the area!
