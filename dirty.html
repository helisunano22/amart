4.4 miles roundtrip

Elevation gain: 1600 feet

Dirty Harry's Balcony

Dirty Harry's Balcony is one of my favorite nearby, easier hikes!
It's located right on I-90, and you're able to see such a nice view of the pass as well as the highwar going through the pass.
Very nice mountains and trees and scenery, always a chill time with friends!
